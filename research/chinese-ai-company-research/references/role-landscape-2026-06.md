## What's NOT a role (per guide §22)

- **Vibe coder** — methodology (Karpathy coined, then self-pivoted to "context engineering"). Not a job.
- **AI-native engineer** — bar/quality, not title. Expected of all engineers increasingly.
- **Loop Engineer** — appears in guide? **NO** — guide last updated 2026-03, Loop Engineering tweet is 2026-06. Below are independent sources, not the guide:
  - Origin: Peter Steinberger tweet 2026-06 (~8M views)
  - Echoed by Boris Cherny (Claude Code lead): "My work now is writing Loops"
  - Addy Osmani (Google) consolidating framework
  - **No LinkedIn job title with "Loop Engineer" found as of 2026-06-23**

## Trend reading (2026-06)

- "Engineer" suffix is winning over "Scientist" suffix for production-side AI work.
- "Harness" / "Context" / "Loop" are **methodology names that travel through blog/tweet → conference talk → spec → maybe Title in 12-24 months**.
- If you see a 2026 JD with "Harness Engineer" or "Loop Engineer" title, it's almost certainly a startup or consulting firm doing it for branding. Tier-1 companies will use Chinese equivalents.
